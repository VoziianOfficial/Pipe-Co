Service overview

Pipe repair requests depend heavily on location and access.

Homeowners may start a pipe repair request because of visible leaking, corrosion, old pipes, wall or ceiling moisture, water pressure changes, damaged exposed lines, or concerns after a freeze, renovation, or inspection.

Pipe & Co does not repair pipes directly. It helps homeowners structure the request and connect with independent providers who may evaluate the issue and discuss quote options.

Pipe location

Tell providers whether the pipe is under a sink, inside a wall, ceiling, basement, crawlspace, slab, or outside.

Visible symptoms

Include dripping, stains, corrosion, pooling water, low pressure, sounds, odor, or recurring moisture.

Access and scope

Access can affect provider conversations, especially when walls, floors, cabinets, or outdoor areas are involved.
