Pipe repair details

Pipe location and access

Mention if the pipe is visible, behind a wall, below flooring, under a sink, outdoors, in a crawlspace, or near a fixture.

Leak or damage signs

Include dripping, corrosion, cracks, stains, water pooling, pressure changes, odors, or sounds near the pipe area.

Repair scope clues

Add pipe age, known material, recent freezing, renovation history, inspection notes, or whether follow-up access work may be needed.
