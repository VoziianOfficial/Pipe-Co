Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Pipe & Co may use cookies and similar technologies to support basic site functionality, preferences, analytics, and user experience.

Last updated: 2026
Legal pages Privacy Policy Cookie Policy Terms of Service

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help a site remember preferences, support basic functionality, and understand how visitors interact with pages.

Platform note: Pipe & Co is an independent provider matching platform. It is not a plumbing contractor and does not perform plumbing work directly.

2. How Pipe & Co may use cookies

Pipe & Co may use cookies or similar technologies for purposes such as:

  • Remembering cookie banner choices.
  • Maintaining basic website functionality.
  • Improving site usability and page performance.
  • Understanding general traffic patterns and page interactions.
  • Supporting security, fraud prevention, or operational diagnostics.

3. Types of cookies

Essential cookies

These cookies support basic site functions, such as remembering your privacy preference choice or enabling core website features.

Preference cookies

These may remember choices you make on the website, such as whether you accepted or declined optional cookies.

Analytics cookies

These may help understand how visitors use the website, which pages are visited, and how the experience can be improved.

Advertising or partner cookies

If used, these cookies may help measure marketing performance or support request matching workflows. Availability and use can depend on the live website configuration.

4. Cookie banner choices

The website may show a cookie banner with options to accept or decline optional cookies. The banner is designed to remain visible until a choice is made. Your selection may be stored in your browser so the site can remember it during future visits.

5. Managing cookies in your browser

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or manage cookies through browser settings. If you block some cookies, parts of the website may not work as expected.

  • You can clear stored cookies from your browser settings.
  • You can block third-party cookies if supported by your browser.
  • You can use private browsing modes to limit local storage of site data.

6. Third-party tools

The website may use third-party libraries, scripts, analytics tools, hosting services, or request-processing partners. These third parties may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

7. Updates to this Cookie Policy

Pipe & Co may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the content on this page.
