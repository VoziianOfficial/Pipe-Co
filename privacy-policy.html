Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Pipe & Co may collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website. Pipe & Co is an independent provider matching platform, not a plumbing contractor.

Last updated: 2026
Legal pages Privacy Policy Cookie Policy Terms of Service

1. Overview

Pipe & Co operates as a free service intended to help homeowners connect with independent local service providers. This website may collect information when a visitor submits a request form, contacts the platform, interacts with website features, or uses basic site preferences.

Important: Pipe & Co does not perform plumbing services directly and does not employ contractors for homeowner projects.

2. Information we may collect

Depending on how you use the website, we may collect information such as:

  • Name and contact details submitted through a form.
  • Phone number, email address, ZIP code, and project notes.
  • Selected plumbing request category.
  • Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, approximate location, and pages visited.
  • Cookie preference choices stored in your browser.

Do not submit sensitive information through the website unless it is necessary for the request you are making.

3. How information may be used

Information submitted through the website may be used to:

  • Process and organize homeowner requests.
  • Help connect users with independent local service providers.
  • Respond to questions or contact requests.
  • Improve site usability, security, and performance.
  • Maintain records for operational, compliance, or legal purposes.

4. Sharing with independent providers

When you submit a request, the information may be shared with independent service providers or related partners for the purpose of responding to your request. These providers are not employees of Pipe & Co.

Homeowners are responsible for verifying provider credentials, license, insurance, pricing, work scope, and all hiring terms before authorizing any work.

5. Cookies and tracking

The website may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences, support basic functionality, analyze usage, and improve the browsing experience. You can review more details on the Cookie Policy page.

6. Data security

Pipe & Co uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect submitted information. However, no website or electronic transmission method can be guaranteed as completely secure.

7. Your choices

You may choose not to submit a request form. You may also adjust cookie settings in your browser or use the cookie banner choices provided on the website.

For privacy-related questions, contact: hello@pipeandco.com.

8. Required disclaimer

Disclaimer: This site is a free service to assist homeowners in connecting with local service providers. All contractors/providers are independent and this site does not warrant or guarantee any work performed. It is the responsibility of the homeowner to verify that the hired contractor furnishes the necessary license and insurance required for the work being performed. All persons depicted in a photo or video are actors or models and not contractors listed on this site.
